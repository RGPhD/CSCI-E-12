  • Level 2 Poses
    • Butterfly Pose
    • A great pose for performing the butterfly action (baddha kona kriya) to assist in opening up the hips and ankles and releasing tension in the lower regions. Beginners can utilize this pose for meditation.
    • Baddha Konasana or Titli Asana
    • Photo retrieved from The Yoga Tutor

    • Burmese and Easy Pose
    • Even though the burmese and the easy pose are the simplest of yoga meditation sitting positions, it may still take a bit of time to feel a sense of ease and yogic steadiness within it for a long period of time. Beginners can utilize this pose for meditation.
    • Sukhasana
    • Photo retrieved from The Yoga Tutor
    • Step-by-Step Instructions at The Yoga Tutor
    • Half Lotus Pose
    • Beginners can utilize the half lotus pose for meditation. This cross-legged meditation and sitting yoga posture is an important preparatory exercise for the attainment of the powerful and well-known padma asana, or "full lotus" of hatha yoga.
