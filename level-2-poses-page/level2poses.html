    • Extended Side Angle Pose
    • The extended side angle pose opens the side of the body from the heel to the fingertips, energizes the body and strengthens the legs and the lungs. Utthita Parsvakonasana utilizes many muscle groups including but not limited to the legs, ankles, groin, chest, shoulders, erector spinae and the abdomen.
    • Utthita Parsvakonasana
    • Photo courtesy of Dr. Robelyn Garcia
    • Step-by-Step Instructions at Yoga Outlet
    • Garland Pose
    • The garland pose stretches and strengthens the feet, ankles, hips, groin, torso and thighs. Malasana, commonly referred to as the wide squat, is a hip-opening posture that helps mobility and balance for daily activities. This pose also tones the abdomen and increases circulation in the pelvis, which can inhance sexual energy and improve concentration.
    • Mālāsana
    • Photo courtesy of Dr. Robelyn Garcia
    • Step-by-Step Instructions at Yoga Outlet
    • Half Lord of The Fishes Pose
    • Half lord of the fishes pose is a popular seated yoga twist that energizes the spine and stimulates digestive fire. Ardha Matsyendrasana increases flexibility in the spine, hips, neck, shoulders and chest while calming the mind and relaxing the nervous system. The moderate to intermediate twist also improves digestion and overall health of your spine.
    • Ardha Matsyendrāsana
    • Photo courtesy of Dr. Robelyn Garcia
    • Step-by-Step Instructions at Yoga Outlet
